above is a selection of analogue photographs from the far away series. this work was completed at björkö kunstnod and funded by the 2023 nordic analog network artist-in-residence program.

during this month long stay i had the privilege of searching for how the past materializes in the present. and for any agency i might have over this process.

my camera is and was my mother's father. the particular history of this camera is seldom spoken of, but lives on for me as incongruent fragments. leaving analogue photography as my means to hold these uncertainties and consider their various faces.
